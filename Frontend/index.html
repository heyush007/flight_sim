About Us

Flight Simulator is a commonly known game with the real-life flight experience. We built a terminal-based flight simulation experience that immerses players in the role of a pilot. The game will simulate real world aviation scenarios with dynamic weather conditions, flight scheduling, arrivals and departures and user progression through increasingly challenging levels. The game is designed to be simple yet encouraging players to engage in the game through complex levels as the players progress further.
